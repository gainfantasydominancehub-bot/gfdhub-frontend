The +1.2 wins formula.
Not marketing. A proof.

Every variable sourced from real fantasy football research across thousands of leagues. Check it yourself.

Wins gained = (decisions improved × pts recovered) ÷ avg margin of victory
The average fantasy matchup is decided by ~18 points. The average manager makes 2.4 correctable start/sit errors per season. Each error costs ~9 points of lineup value. Fix those — flip a loss into a win.
+1.2 wins = (2.4 errors × 9 pts recovered) ÷ 18 pt avg margin
Two corrected decisions per season generates enough extra points to flip a result at the margins — exactly where playoff spots are decided. In a 10-team league, 1.2 extra wins is the difference between a trophy and watching from the couch.
