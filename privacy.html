Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026  ·  Digital Marketing Gen, Inc. · Great Neck, NY  ·  support@gfdhub.com

The short version: We collect only what we need to run GFDHub. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information — including your phone number — to any third party, ever. SMS opt-in data is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Who We Are

GFDHub is operated by Digital Marketing Gen, Inc., a New York corporation located in Great Neck, NY. References to "GFDHub," "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to Digital Marketing Gen, Inc. You can reach us at support@gfdhub.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of GFDHub:

  • Email address — collected when you join our waitlist, create an account, or complete a purchase
  • Phone number — collected only when you voluntarily opt in to receive SMS messages from the GFDHub AI Offensive Coordinator SMS service
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or payment details on our servers
  • Fantasy football context — league format, roster information, and questions you submit through the AI OC chat interface, used solely to provide you with personalized responses
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and session information collected via Google Analytics (anonymized)
  • Communications — if you contact us for support, we retain that correspondence

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your GFDHub account
  • Process your payment and fulfill your subscription
  • Deliver the AI Offensive Coordinator service, including SMS responses
  • Send transactional messages about your account (receipts, password resets, service updates)
  • Send SMS messages you have opted in to receive, including AI lineup recommendations and NFL updates
  • Improve the GFDHub product and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. SMS Messaging — A2P Compliance

Your phone number is never sold, shared, or rented to third parties for marketing purposes. SMS opt-in consent is not transferable and is used exclusively to deliver the GFDHub AI OC service you signed up for.

By providing your phone number and opting in to GFDHub SMS messaging, you consent to receive text messages from GFDHub, including:

  • AI Offensive Coordinator lineup recommendations in response to your questions
  • NFL injury alerts and fantasy-relevant updates you have requested
  • Transactional messages related to your GFDHub account

Message frequency varies based on your usage and the NFL schedule. During the regular season, you may receive multiple messages per week.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time. You will receive one confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent. You can also opt out by emailing support@gfdhub.com.

To get help: Reply HELP to any SMS message or email support@gfdhub.com.

Opting out of SMS messages does not cancel your GFDHub subscription. You will retain full access to the web-based AI OC at gfdhub.com.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — we use Stripe (payments), Telnyx (SMS delivery), Railway (hosting), and Google Analytics (usage analytics). These providers access your data only to perform services on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from using it for other purposes
  • Anthropic — questions you submit to the AI OC are processed by Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of this data
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers — if Digital Marketing Gen, Inc. is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified via email

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes (e.g., Stripe transaction records).

AI OC conversation history is retained for up to 12 months to provide personalized context across sessions. You may request deletion of your conversation history at any time by contacting support@gfdhub.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking

GFDHub uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site usage via Google Analytics (anonymized IP)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some functionality of GFDHub.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure database connections, and restricted access controls. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe and is PCI-DSS compliant. We do not store credit card data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security but take reasonable precautions to protect your data.

9. Children's Privacy

GFDHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at support@gfdhub.com.

10. Your Rights

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

To exercise any of these rights, email support@gfdhub.com. We will respond within 30 days.
